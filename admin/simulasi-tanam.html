🧮 Metodologi Perhitungan

1. Fuzzy Logic Scoring

Menggunakan fungsi keanggotaan fuzzy untuk menilai setiap parameter:

Score = (suhu_fuzzy × 0.25) + (curah_hujan_fuzzy × 0.30) + (pH_fuzzy × 0.20) + (kelembaban_fuzzy × 0.15) + (air_fuzzy × 0.10)

2. TOPSIS Method

Technique for Order of Preference by Similarity to Ideal Solution:

TOPSIS_Score = D⁻ / (D⁺ + D⁻)
D⁺ = Jarak ke solusi ideal positif
D⁻ = Jarak ke solusi ideal negatif

3. Probabilistic Scoring

Menghitung probabilitas kesuksesan berdasarkan data historis:

P(success) = Σ(kondisi_sesuai × bobot_parameter) / total_bobot
